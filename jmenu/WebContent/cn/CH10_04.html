10.4.1 x $ y

　

"ABC" $ "DEF"     Outcome  "ABCDEF"

"  AB" $ "DE"        Outcome "  ABDE"

"ABC " $ 123        Outcome "ABC123"

"  ABC   " $123     Outcome "  ABC   123"

"ABC" $ -123        Outcome  "ABC-123"

　

　

▲Top

　

10.4.2 x[y]

　

"ABC" [ 1 ]       Outcome "A"

123 [ 2 ]            Outcome 2

-123  [ 1 ]          Outcome "–"

0.142 [ 2 ]          Outcome "."

-0.142 [ 4 ]        Outcome 1

　

　

▲Top

　

10.4.3 x[y to z]

　

"ABCDEF" [ 2 TO 4 ]     Outcome "BCD"

123 [ 2 TO 3]                  Outcome 23

-123  [ 1 TO 3 ]               Outcome -12

0.142 [ 2 TO 4 ]              Outcome .14

-0.142 [ 1 TO 4 ]             Outcome -0.1
